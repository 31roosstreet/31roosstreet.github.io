Cozy Family Cottage in Onrust

A rustic retreat for families and friends

About the Cottage

This charming cottage sleeps 6 people comfortably, with one double room, one room with a double bed and a single bed, and one single room. There's also an infant cot available.

It's pet-friendly, and just a 5-10 minute walk from the beach. The cottage features a gas stove, dishwasher, washing machine, Weber braai, and a cozy fireplace.

Rates and Availability

Friends rates: R650/night for up to 4 people, extra R100/night per person for up to 6 people. Cleaning fee: R320. Minimum 2-night stay preferred.

Location

Our cottage is located in Onrust, just 15 minutes from Hermanus and 10 minutes from the beautiful Hemel en Aarde wine route. Enjoy nearby hikes, coastal paths, and tidal pools within walking distance. Onrust village offers an artisan sourdough bakery, farm stall, and lovely restaurants.
